Race information

In 2021 Diane's Run is held in Tuesday, August 31st in Cheyenne Starting at Prairie Park. All race fees are non-refundable and non-transferable, even in the event of inclement weather. Two races are offered: a 5k (3.1 miles) and a 10k (6.2 miles) event. We also offer a mile Family Fun Walk.

Race Times

  • 5k Race starts promptly at 10 a.m.
  • 10k Race starts promptly at 11 a.m.
  • Family Fun Walk starts at noon.
  • Celebration lunch and reception opens at 1:00 p.m
